Pizza Dough

Home

Make pizza dough with rapid-rise yeast instead of buying pre-made dough at the grocery store. It's quick and easy to make from scratch, and it tastes better too.

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour, or as needed
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • 1 envelope Fleischmann's® RapidRise Yeast*
  • 1 cup very warm water (120 degrees F to 130 degrees F)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  1. Combine 2 cups flour, salt, and yeast in a large bowl.
  2. Stir in very warm water and oil until combined,
  3. then stir in just enough of remaining 1/2 cup flour to make a soft dough.
  4. Knead on a lightly floured surface until smooth and elastic, 4 to 6 minutes.
  5. Cover and let rest for 10 minutes.
